Hey, I'm Evan Nelson

I’ve always believed that design isn’t just about what you see it’s about what you feel. I grew up in a single-parent home, watching my mom work tirelessly to keep us going. That drive to build, to create something meaningful from nothing, became the foundation of who I am.

When I met my wife, I realized it was time to chase what truly set my soul on fire, creating intuitive experiences through design and technology. Every pixel, every layout, every motion tells a story, and I love being the one who brings that story to life.

My passion lives at the intersection of web development, UI/UX design, and graphic design. Whether I’m coding a responsive interface, refining a color palette, or crafting brand visuals, I pour everything into making sure the final product feels effortless, immersive, and alive.

The projects you’ll find here aren’t just assignments, they’re snapshots of how I think, how I grow, and how deeply I care about creating work that connects people to design in a meaningful way.
